Katherine Xu

I'm a CS PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania GRASP Lab, where I'm fortunate to be advised by Professor Jianbo Shi. My research interests include image/video/3D generation, scene understanding, and multimodal learning.

I completed my bachelor's and master's in Computer Science with a minor in Math at MIT, where I worked with multiple research groups at MIT CSAIL, MIT Quest for Intelligence, and MIT Environmental Solutions Initiative. I've also had the pleasure of interning at Meta for two summers and at the Honda Research Institute.
