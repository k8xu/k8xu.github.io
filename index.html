Modeling Extreme Heat Risk in Urban Areas Using Computer Vision and Data Analysis

Katherine Xu
MIT MEng Thesis, 2023
Writing a paper to submit to a workshop or journal

I developed a weighted sum model that estimates the extreme heat risk of an urban area at the granular census tract level. To construct this model, I fine-tuned a vision transformer for semantic segmentation to extract relevant risk factors from aerial scene images. I also incorporated additional heat hazard and heat vulnerability factors from land surface temperature, building, and socioeconomic datasets. As a proof of concept, this research focuses on developing a heat risk model for Boston, which experiences intense urban heat islands.
