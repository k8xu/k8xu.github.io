Kate Xu

I'm Kate, a senior at MIT studying Computer Science and Engineering. Currently, I am examining the biases of generative models and de-biasing these models with Professor Phillip Isola and Dr. Swami Sankaranarayanan.

Previously, I leveraged computer vision for image segmentation using liver biopsy images with Professor Nicholas Roy and Katherine Fairchild at the MIT Quest for Intelligence. I also developed a graph database for cyber threat hunting with Dr. Una-May O'Reilly and Dr. Erik Hemberg in the Anyscale Learning for All (ALFA) Group at MIT CSAIL.
